IoTify 2500 fuel service stations for a global energy company

This project required that our team create a new interface on the SAP cloud platform that met the objectives of the client's then-current solution and prepared them for future enhancements on the SAP cloud platform. Applications displayed IoT data and selectively presented site IoT objects (over three hundred thousand) geographically against a map of the world for twenty-three countries in four languages.

IoTistics handled all application prototyping, authentication, authorization, Portal, IoT, API, onboarding and reporting aspects of the project.
